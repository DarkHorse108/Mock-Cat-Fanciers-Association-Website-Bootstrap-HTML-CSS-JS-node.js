Official Breeds

Currently, the Cat Fancier's Association officially recognizes 42 feline breeds that are eligible for competition in the Champion Class. Of all the breeds, the top 3 most prominent that have been in competition for over a century include the Persian, British Shorthair, and Ragdoll.
