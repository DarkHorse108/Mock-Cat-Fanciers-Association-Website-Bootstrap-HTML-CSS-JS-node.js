Marshmallow, Siamese Mix

Cat Care

source
  • At minimum, an enclosed space/home that is dry and protected from the elements should be provided for your cat.

  • Give clean and fresh water in a designated bowl, be sure to wash the bowl at the end of each day as well.

  • Fresh food should be provided daily in a separate bowl from that used for water.

  • Keep the dishes separated as to not have food accidentally contaminating water bowls, and clean daily.

  • A litter box should be placed in a quiet space away from the housing structure and food/water dishes.

  • The litter should be scooped/filtered daily and topped off with fresh litter. Entire litterbox should be replaced weekly at minimum.

  • Cats should at minimum visit their veterinarian every twelve months.

  • Any signs of unusual behavior or illness, such as excessive vomiting, urination, etc. warrants an immediate medical visit.
